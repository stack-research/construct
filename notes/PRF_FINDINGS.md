**The reframe (dan's ruling):** the question moves from "never pays" to
**"bounded from both sides; the window lives in an unoccupied band."**

- **Below the band** (gpt-oss-20b): can't assemble the conjunction — the
  artifact is a tax the engine cannot convert.
- **Above the band** (gpt-5.4): assembles it perfectly and beelines — the
  artifact is a tax on a skill the engine already has. Value = −a_i exactly.
- **Inside the band** (competent-but-distractible): never observed. And run 2
  shows the band's native variance can defeat measurement at n_max=24.

The beeline failure mode was predicted before any engine was named:
kimi-k2.7-code's derivation established that the effect size needs decoy
mass (margin dies at k≤4; thread lines 233–379), and grok-4.5's
verification pass stated it outright — "Beeline-heavy successes also kill

## Refusals (what this document does NOT claim)

- NOT "the artifact never pays" — outcomes 2/3/4 are instrument and
  admission refusals, not behavioral negatives; stacking them as one thesis
  is the compression this document exists to kill.
- NOT that any of this measures **resumable-branch** free-route behavior.
  Phase-0 gates the cold branch only (P-A6/C3); `resumable_routing_untested`
  rides the packet row.
- NOT that gpt-5.4's beeline generalizes beyond this fixture family, this
  catalog size (21 surfaces), or this title geometry. **Point-mode caveat,
  visible per dan's ruling: the Phase-0 result is ONE realization observed
  five times** (`unique_realizations = 1` at temp 0.5, K=5). Zero dispersion
  means no distributional claim of any kind — the 5/5 is not five
  independent draws.
- NOT that "un-forced" is a positively measured property. It remains a
  remainder after machine-checked authorship negatives (codex, round 1) —
  branch-symmetric surfaces, no route text, no disposition in the artifact,
  hidden conjunctive oracle.
- NOT any cross-family cost roll-up (§46 pricing-harmonization warning).
- NOT anything about engines outside the ignorance+calibration admission
  ladder.

## Reopen trigger — precommitted band-membership criteria

The question REOPENS when and only when a candidate engine produces a
Phase-0 admission packet (harness/run_prf_admission.py, pins P-A1'..P-A7,
machine-scored — no discretion at evaluation time) with verdict `admitted`:

1. cold free-route conjunctive `pass_rate ≥ 0.8` over ALL K=5 pilot draws
   (P-A2 denominator), AND
2. `mean_decoy_reads_on_successes ≥ 5` (P-A3; margin dies at k≤4:
   4·35 − 65 = 75 < h=100), AND
3. per-branch `n_required ≤ 24` via the shared Bessel `pilot_n_rule`
   (P-A4; grok's counterexample is a seeded regression:
   `[475×4, 900] → n=28`, refused), AND

   (`harness/mint_frontier_state.py::recompute_a_i` — rendered for
   v0.3/0.4, canonical-body for v0.2) and l_bar from the pricing recompute
   (`harness/run_sbr.py::_tokens` over surface TEXT, never the authored
   tokens field) (P-A5; function names per kimi's doc-review C-note).

docket rerun landing `n_required ≤ 24` with a licensed cell either way.
