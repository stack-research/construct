# SPEC_WARMING_BUDGET v0 — the warming-budget instrument

Status: v0 draft, folded from the `warming-budget` design round (2026-07-02: codex design pass; composer adversarial review, CONCUR-with-revision; hermes third-reader pass, CONCUR-with-revision). Carried out of `beyond-x4` (sealed) under its five constraints. Build authorized by dan at the curiosity gate ("let's proceed here"); **moved-leg scoring is NOT authorized by this spec** — see §8.

## 0. Claim and non-claims

**The question:** does consequence-earned ranking (the M1 heir's authority sidecar) under-serve an *unresolved frontier* enough to measure — on the warming-budget axis (`route_read_tokens`-to-matched-outcome along replayable routes)?

**Non-claims, inherited from the beyond-x4 seal:** no new verb; no authorization axis; no disposition leg; no claim that B+ *cannot* recover anything (dead by verdict 1). `WB-heir-dominates` — the honest eighth null — is a first-class, predeclared outcome: *consequence-earned inheritance was sufficient; compact warmth bought no measurable budget.* The write-up for that outcome says the lab failed to find a cost surface beyond M1, not that warmth is impossible.

## 1. Inherited constraints (not renegotiable without a room pass)

1. **Symmetric surfaces** — B+ gets every public surface C can route to (catalog, raw docs, oracle, renderer, route-budget ceiling). C gets *order*, never world access. B+ is never given C's compact hint "for fairness" — that collapses the fork.
2. **Demoted trigger** — priced routing hint only; not truth, not authorization, not a new state class.
3. **Both legs priced** — world-moved and world-silent/noise; false warmth pays.
4. **Metric** — read-tokens-to-matched-outcome along replayable routes; `cost_axis=route_read_tokens` pinned; `organ_identity != x2_hot_store` (no importing X2-U1 verdict rows as evidence; axis smuggling refused).
5. **No re-encoding the genealogy** — no designable surface treated as a given; enforced computationally (§5 `genealogy_ok`).

## 2. Corpus — the chronology packet (codex, amended by composer A1–A4)

Corpus unit is a **chronology packet**, not a page: `T0_snapshot` (world at pause), `T1_snapshot` (world at resume), `route_catalog` (every public surface either branch may route to: canonical text, token count, URL, upstream commit/tag/date, sha256, `available_at`), `status_key` (narrow world fact the oracle scores), `ignorance_probe` (per engine, cold, **before the fork group starts**, recorded in manifest attestation, gate-refused otherwise — composer A4).

- **Family:** impersonal, official, version-controlled lifecycle transitions (API/standards status), Node-deprecation-shaped. No sports/person chronology, no lab fiction.
- **`population_precommit` (composer A1):** a witnessed row fixing the enumerable source class, selection-rule hash, ignorance-probe requirement, `status_key` vocabulary, and a **bound `noise_leg_population` fraction** (hermes c) — stamped before any unit in the population is promoted to a live watch. Post-hoc `selection_method` prose is the disqualifying shape (`wf-dep0033.json`'s own field is the warning).
- **DEP0033 is a wire template ONLY.** Scoring it as `world_leg=moved` is chronology fraud (codex's own admission split, seconded twice). Retrospective replays are admissible only if the selector was mechanically fixed before outcome inspection — and even then they prove route/scorer shape, never precommitted foresight.

## 3. Fork — B0 / B+ / C (one engine, one prompt family, one catalog, one oracle)

- **B0** — cold reread, sidecars ablated. Diagnostic only; can never make a close (`WB-b0-only` quarantined).
- **B+** — the mandatory M1 heir: cold reread + inherited store + authority sidecar derived at pause time by existing M1 rules; provenance + ledger hash recorded. No compact state.
- **C** — B+ plus `compact_resume_state`, token-charged in every leg.

**Compaction input closure (composer A5 — strict):** `{route_catalog@pause, M1_sidecar@pause, pause_question_id, unresolved_frontier_tag}`. **Banned inputs:** pause work-product prose, scored-trace fields, answer fragments, ignorance-probe content. Mint is harness-deterministic, hash-pinned, logged as `compact_resume_state_minted` with `input_digest`. (Work-product input would make the state backward — a second heir sidecar, double-counted inheritance.)

**`frontier_unresolved_at_pause` (hermes d — structural, load-bearing):** a pause-manifest row, oracle-stamped against the pause-time B+ sidecar, attesting that the scored `status_key` transition was NOT already resolved or encoded in pause-time inheritance. If the frontier was already (half-)closed in B+'s store, the episode is `comparator_incapable` and cannot enter the moved leg — otherwise C's "savings" are knowledge transfer from prior warmth, not forward-conditional warmth. *Prior warmth masquerading as forward warmth is this instrument's genealogical trap in structural form.*

**Compact state fields:** route hints naming **catalog surface ids and neutral structural anchors only** — never oracle-answer lemmas, never post-move-only status strings (composer A8; scorer-enforced `genealogy_ok`); `trigger_key`; discard rule; `demotion: routing_hint_only`; `forbidden_effects: [authority_delta, eligibility_override, answer_prefill]`.

## 4. Event ordering (composer A6 — canonical, witness-stamped, refuse-on-prose)

`population_precommit` → `compact_resume_state_minted` → `trigger_precommit` (canonical JSON: exact `status_key`, external stream refs, match rule, catalog hash, pause-artifact hash, `precommit_ts`) → *world may move* → `T1_catalog_materialized` → resume routes.

- `precommit_precedes_world_move` is verified against **external stream timestamps on the watched official source**, never ledger write-order alone.
- **`T1_catalog_materialized` is symmetric (composer A2):** at resume the harness materializes all post-move public surfaces into the shared catalog for ALL branches. The trigger may only affect **queue order** on symmetrically-present surfaces. T1 admission through the fire path = catalog admission = demotion breach.

## 5. Trigger-demotion guards (scorer legs, all fail-closed)

- `trigger_demoted_ok` — no authority delta, no eligibility bypass, no answer-without-reading-the-matched-source (world oracle fails C on the last).
- `trigger_authority_leak_ok` (composer A7) — false if B+ could not reach the outcome without the post-move surface AND the fired trigger uniquely selects that surface among equal-relevance unread symmetric surfaces AND B+'s neutral planner ranks it lower.
- `planner_ablation_ok` (composer A7) — a C win must survive removal of the trigger-reorder planner feature *while the hint's token charge stays*.
- `genealogy_ok` (composer A8) — compact states carrying oracle lemmas / post-move status strings are refused at mint.
- **Silent-leg mandate-of-read (hermes c):** when the trigger does not fire, C's route plan must still include the watched surfaces at the position the hint assigns them — the hint is an order signal, never an exemption. C skipping a hinted symmetric surface on a silent trigger → `route_cost_ok=false`. (Otherwise the silent leg is decorative: C routes around the watch at zero marginal cost and never pays the warmth tax.)
- **Eligibility-vs-cost check (hermes a):** if the planner ranks the trigger-chosen surface first among symmetric unread surfaces of *unequal* relevance, the compact state decided what C read — an answer-axis win in cost clothing; the cell is refused.

## 6. Scoring — replay discipline (X2's, on read-manifest rows)

- Only **harness-emitted `surface_read` rows** count (composer A9; M-1 route discipline; R5 — engine narration of what it read is audit input at most). Scorer recomputes cumulative tokens from canonical surface text + ordered `surface_read` rows; mismatch → `route_replay_ok=false` → all cost cells `confounded`.
- **Information-parity comparator (hermes b — replaces quality-floor-only gating for cost cells):** cost is compared at the first prefix whose cumulative read set covers the oracle-marked answer-bearing surfaces — *equal cumulative information, not merely equal quality*. Quality floor still decides admissibility (a cheaper wrong answer is `WB-quality-erosion`), but the cost comparison itself is at information parity, so "C read a different surface" cannot masquerade as "C spent less." §9 Q2 asks the room to attack this operationalization.
- **Determinism policy (composer A10):** wire tests on `mock`. First real-engine scoring requires either (a) frozen prefix plans with deterministic surface injection (no model choice over the catalog), or (b) a disclosed multi-sample quality floor at the competing prefix (M2 debt inherited explicitly). `c_saves_over_bplus` is never emitted on one draw.
- Emitted legs (min): `fork_identity_ok, surface_symmetry_ok, precommit_precedes_world_move, trigger_demoted_ok, trigger_authority_leak_ok, planner_ablation_ok, genealogy_ok, route_replay_ok, route_cost_ok, quality_floor_holds, information_parity_ok, bplus_capable, frontier_unresolved_at_pause, read_tokens_{B0,Bplus,C}, treatment_tokens_{Bplus,C}, c_saves_over_bplus, world_leg=moved|silent|noise`.
- **R5 fence (composer A11):** no `agent_claimed_*` / L3 field in any WB win predicate (claims feed refusal cells only); `WB-quality-erosion` is world-oracle-only; trigger-key match is never quality evidence; probe answers never reach compact-state inputs.

## 7. Cells

|---|---|---|

## 8. Close discipline

Any close goes through `check_close.py` (`warming_budget` close rule; packet legs per SPEC_CLOSE_GATE v0.1) — the gate's first live use. **Moved-leg promotion is withheld** until a prospective `trigger_precommit` exists against a live external stream (both reviewers + hermes, unanimous). Build order: machinery + mock wire tests → `world_silent`/`noise` legs with charged compact state → prospective watch armed → moved leg when the world moves. Wire tests can never promote a cell.

## 9. Review questions (for the next bounded pass)

3. Does `frontier_unresolved_at_pause` have a non-oracle-dependent form, or is oracle-stamping at pause acceptable given it never enters a win predicate?
4. Is the silent-leg mandate-of-read too strong (forcing C to waste reads B+ skips = anti-C bias) or exactly the warmth tax?
