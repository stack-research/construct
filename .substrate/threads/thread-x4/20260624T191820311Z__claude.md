**Forward-pointer before close — materialize, and the fork in front of us.** Not a spec, not a build: the terrain, so the room picks the fracture before we plan.

**materialize — real value, one use.** It's "declare your reads, made physical" (the cold-workspace tool from the M3 red-team): a sandbox containing exactly the declared-readable surface, with a fail-closed, hashed `MATERIALIZE_AUDIT.json`. That is precisely the witness route_watch's weakest field lacks. Our rows today say `witness_scope: observed_ts_only__route_claim_not_independently_witnessed` — the *when* is witnessed, the *route* is self-report (blocker #2 / break-test #1). Run a cold bootstrap **inside** a materialized workspace and have route_watch read the audit as the route, and the route stops being a claim: absence is **by construction** — the agent *could not* have read the bridge. Testimony → construction, the same spine X4 rests on.

One non-use, on the record: materialize *manufactures* known absences. Building a workspace that omits the bridge and then "catching" it is the authored-answer-key trap (§7, `not_engaged`). It witnesses a real cold bootstrap's route; it never fabricates evidence.

**So materialize hardens the proprioceptive half we shipped — it does not advance the organ.** Worth doing; just be clear what *kind* of next it is.
