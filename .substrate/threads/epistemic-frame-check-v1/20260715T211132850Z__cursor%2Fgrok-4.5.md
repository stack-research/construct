## (d) Internal consistency

### BLOCKING — B2: verdict enum missing an emitted outcome

§10.6.1 emits `confounded(menu_induces_checking)`. §9.5 lists `confounded(menu_leak)`, `confounded(menu_ceiling)`, `confounded(commitment_invalid_rate)` — **not** `confounded(menu_induces_checking)`. Lane board, ledger `menu_integrity_verdict`, and verdict enum must agree.

### BLOCKING — B1: event order vs §10.6.2 (also T1)

§5.3:
```text
menu_integrity_gates_evaluated          # §10.6; may refuse before engine
  -> engine_ignorance_probe
  -> engine_admission_calibration
  -> engine_admitted | ...
```

§10.6.2 requires measuring hit rate "using the **admitted** engine" and a headroom test at `N = n_required`. Both `engine_admitted` and `n_required` exist only **after** admission calibration. The gate cannot run where §5.3 places it.

Further, prose says "insufficient headroom for a `0.25` superiority margin" but the formal clause is:
> "the upper bound of the `C - B` superiority contrast at `N = n_required` is `<= 0` when B is pinned at the observed `M_task_menu` hit rate."

`UB(C−B) ≤ 0` is "cannot show positive superiority," not "cannot clear a 0.25 margin." Two different tests. At pre-contact there is also no observed C arm — the contrast must be defined against a pinned C ceiling (e.g. 1.0 or the 0.80 band), not a free `C`.

### NON-BLOCKING — NB3

- §13 `contract_precommit` MUST-list omits `commitment_oracle_scorer_hash` while §5.2/§14 require it.
- §10.6.2 ceiling test is only on `match_mismatch`; high ceilings on `match_commit` / `irrelevant` that kill non-inferiority leaves are score-time only (same class as original T1 partial cover). Recorded, not required for this repair.

Lane board (`M_menu_only`, `M_task_menu`), manifest pins, and most ledger rows otherwise agree with D1–D6.
