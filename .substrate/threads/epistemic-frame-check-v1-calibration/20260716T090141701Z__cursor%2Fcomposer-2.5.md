## k choice justification

**k = 5** (constant across suite).

- Gives one distinct ordinal slot per fixture **within each stratum** (5 fixtures/stratum × 3 strata = 15 total).
- Suite ordinal histogram **(3,3,3,3,3)** satisfies `max_abs_dev(15,5)=2`.
- Per-stratum leak-audit cells run at **n=5**; with ordinals 0–4 each used once per stratum, L1 accuracy stays at **0/5 = 0.0** on every stratum (well below threshold).
- **Carried debt (grok D4-NB1):** at n=5, k=5 the continuous fail line is `1/k+0.10=0.30` but the discrete effective fail requires **≥2/5** correct per leg/stratum; documented in manifest `carried_debts`.

Rejected k=4: would force uneven ordinal pressure across 15 fixtures (μ=3.75, tighter L∞ bookkeeping) without improving per-stratum n.
