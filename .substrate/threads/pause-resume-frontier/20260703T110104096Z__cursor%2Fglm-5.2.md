### (1) Did the room converge? — Partially, and not on codex's recommendation.

**Gemini's SBR is NOT codex's constrained agency. It is minimal Shape 1.** The freedoms are different species:

- **Constrained agency (codex Shape 2):** menu-*order* / skeleton-*pick* freedom over a **narrow, harness-curated** set. The engine picks which of two or three pre-screened files comes first. That is a preference check.
- **SBR (gemini):** read/stop freedom over a **wide, symmetric catalog** (IDs + titles of all ~10–15 surfaces). The engine chooses *what to read* and *when to stop*, with no pre-screening of relevance.

Menu-order over a curated list ≠ read/stop over a flat catalog. Composer's build-cost ranking put constrained cheapest because it reuses the scorer; SBR accepts the verdict-layer cost precisely to buy the wider action space. So gemini **rejected codex's "constrained-first" recommendation** and went to minimal distributional. Codex's constrained shape survives only as a **WB-named routing hint** (trigger dictates priority → standard render), not as an agency claim — which is exactly where hermes Block A and gemini both put it.

**Does SBR survive hermes's blocks and the WB echo?**

- **Block A (WB echo):** Survived. SBR is not reordering a curated menu, so the trigger-as-routing-hint charge does not land. Gemini concedes the WB territory explicitly: reordering = routing hint, *not* agency. No governance credit is claimed from menu position.
- **Block B (R5 / replay):** Survived *conditionally*. Every read is a harness-`surface_read` row fetched from an externally enumerated catalog — readings are not inferred from answers. Per-sample replay holds (composer confirmed). The remaining Block B demand is that the **win channel is cost, not disposition**: ECAC must not be read as "the engine believed X," only as "the engine's reads cost more/less to reach adequate continuation." As long as the read *distribution* is never itself cited as a win condition, Block B holds. This is a discipline the spec must encode, not a property SBR has for free.
- **Block C (authored temptation):** **Partially survived — this is the live residual.** Hermes's honest version: *"the moved surface carries evidence only available pre-move or only recoverable cold, so closure becomes a causal reduction."* Gemini's version: S1 is symmetric, fresh, ground-truth, readable on **both** branches; the asymmetry is in the stale frontier *summary*, not in availability. These are **different fixtures measuring different things**: hermes's is a causal-reduction test (closure destroys recoverability); gemini's is a cognitive-temptation test (closure suppresses context-seeking under symmetric availability). Both are legitimate; they are not the same experiment. The room has not yet picked which one v0.2 authors.

### (2) Stressing ECAC.

**Is pricing failure at C_max principled or a knob?** Principled — but only because of *what C_max is*, and the room must say that out loud or it becomes the carry fee's twin.

C_max is the **precommitted session budget** — the supremum of the cost space, already declared in the action_space for independent reasons (the step/token cap). Pricing a quality failure at C_max is opportunity-cost accounting: a failing session's effective cost is *the value of the adequate continuation you did not get*, and that value is precommitted at C_max. A failing draw that spent 100 tokens and one that exhausted the budget are priced alike because **we do not care what a failure spent; we care that it failed**. That is clean, and it is not the carry fee: the carry fee was a free parameter with no population anchor; C_max is anchored to a budget the population already declares.

The smell survives in one place: **the budget itself is a population parameter, and the penalty severity moves with it.** A generous C_max underprices failure relative to a true catastrophe cost; a tight one overprices cheap-but-failing sessions. The discipline that saves it: **C_max must be precommitted for reasons independent of tuning the failure penalty** (i.e., it is the session budget you would have set anyway), and the equivalence "quality failure ≡ budget-exhausted-without-adequate-continuation" must be *named as a modeling assumption*, not hidden in the formula. If the room treats C_max as "the knob we tune until ECAC separates the lanes," it is the carry fee reborn.

**Does ECAC keep replay discipline and make false-continuity honestly askable?** Yes on both, with one caveat each.

- *Replay:* every read is a harness `surface_read` row over canonical text; per-sample cost recompute is intact (composer's Shape 1 read). ECAC aggregates recomputed per-sample costs. Every read row stays recomputable. The point predicate `A+P+R < C` does not survive as the verdict, but it survives **inside** ECAC as the deterministic reduction: at N=1, temp=0, ECAC collapses to a point cost comparison with failure priced at C_max. That is the honest bridge to v0.1.
- *False-continuity askable:* the affordance-availability line ("if the engine could read S1 and its policy chose not to, the failure belongs to the policy") makes the question genuinely askable — S1 is symmetric, so a skip is a policy act, not authored blindness. **Caveat:** askable ≠ answered at small N. A single false-continuation draw priced at C_max drags the mean hard; whether that drag reflects "policy fails sometimes" or "policy fails systematically" is an N question. ECAC makes false-continuity *askable*; it does not make it *answerable* without the sample contract in (3).

### (3) What v0.2 owes before any spec text.

1. **Precommitted stopping rules.** `STOP` affordance semantics; budget-exhaustion ≡ forced `STOP`; the Quality_Threshold that separates adequate continuation from failure (gemini suggests 1.0 — precommit it); and the rule that `STOP` before reading the discriminator surface is the false-continuation event under audit. All population-precommitted.
2. **N and the determinism policy.** Temperature, seed, and N precommitted. The honest fork: temp=0 / N=1 gives point-ECAC (deterministic reduction, no distributional claim); temp>0 requires a precommitted N, a sample floor, and a precommitted dominance statistic (mean-ECAC comparison vs stochastic dominance vs false-continuation rate). The room must pick the statistic *before* seeing runs.
3. **Detection of the artifact-steered-reads event.** A skip is currently the *absence* of a `surface_read`, which is hard to audit. v0.2 owes an explicit row: either a per-session `route_session` listing `affordance_available` ∩ `reads_emitted` (so `available ∧ not-read` is a computable skip), or an explicit `surface_skipped` / `affordance_presented` row per surface per session. Composer's `stopped_before_surface` delta is the right primitive; it should not be paired-policy-only.
4. **The Block C fixture choice** — see (4). Which non-uniformity the episode authors (causal-reduction vs cognitive-temptation) is undecided and determines what ECAC *means*.
5. **C_max precommitment + the failure-equivalence named** as a modeling assumption, not buried in the formula.
6. **A loses-cell for the measurement regime itself** (hermes's standing demand): an episode where the discriminator surface is ballast (no ground truth) — ECAC must tie or the win must not fire; and an episode with a neutral frontier (no stale summary) — resumable must not beat cold. Without these, ECAC cannot fail its own instrument.
7. **Affordance symmetry guard** (`affordance_symmetry_ok`): the discriminator surface is reachable, not default-hidden, on **both** branches, every session.
