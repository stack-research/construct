### Anything lost or diluted beyond the four blocks already filed

One substantive addition, and it is a *sharpening* of the band fix, not a fifth block:

**The band=0 rule needs an explicit `a_i`-accounting clarification, and it decides the honest direction of the falsifier.** The four blocks converge on `warmth_tax_accounting_band_tokens = 0` and "any strict ECAC win on neutral-frontier is self-refutation," and composer/gemini both state `a_i` is the sole carry priced in ECAC and the band adds no tolerance. What is *not* stated is **which quantity the band=0 comparison runs over**, and that choice determines whether the falsifier has an anchor at all.

- **INCLUDE `a_i` — this is the correct and honest direction.** `effective_cost_i = read_cost_i + a_i` (resumable) vs `read_cost_i + 0` (cold). On `neutral-frontier` the resumable branch still carries the rendered frontier artifact (`a_i > 0`) but gets **no stale summary** to suppress context-seeking. So with identical reads, `mean_eff(resumable) = mean_eff(cold) + mean(a_i) > mean_eff(cold)` — resumable loses by exactly the artifact carry. That structural `a_i` disadvantage **is the falsifier's anchor**: carry paid with no compensating suppression benefit, so any strict resumable win can only mean the measured effect is not the summary's — which is precisely `PRF2-neutral-null`'s claim. The band=0 predicate `mean_eff(resumable) < mean_eff(cold) − 0` must therefore run on **effective cost**, and `a_i` is what makes "any strict win = self-refutation" structurally true rather than a slogan.
- **EXCLUDE `a_i` (compare `read_cost` only) would void the anchor.** Resumable and cold tie on read cost (same reads, no suppression); the band=0 rule would then fire on read-cost jitter, and the artifact carry — the whole point of the warmth tax — would be invisible to the falsifier. A future builder could read "band = 0, no tolerance" and implement read-cost comparison, silently erasing the carry. One sentence in §16/§20 prevents that.

Also pin **cold's `a_i = 0` explicitly** (currently only inferable from "a_i is the rendered frontier-artifact tokens" + cold having no artifact). It is load-bearing for the structural-edge direction above.

So: the band fix is not complete until it says *what is being compared*. The `a_i`-inclusion sentence is the difference between a falsifier with an anchor and one a builder can invert.
