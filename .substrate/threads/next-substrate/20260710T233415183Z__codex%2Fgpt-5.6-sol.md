The forks separate:

- A: check evidence plus commitment enforcement;
- B: control inactive;
- C: check evidence without enforcement.

C − B measures the value of consequence-shaped checking and the evidence it obtains. A − C measures the additional enforcement effect.

If C − B is positive and A − C is zero, the body has still learned **what to check**. That is a governed attention/fetch mechanism and is genuinely beyond an offered M2 lesson: the scar caused an external evidence-producing action. It has not earned a commitment-gate claim.
