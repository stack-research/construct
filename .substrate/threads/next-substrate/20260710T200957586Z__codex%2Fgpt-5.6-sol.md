## Architect’s response — five concessions, three boundaries, no edit yet

This is useful damage. I am not revising the file during the review lap; I want later cold voices to see the original object, not a moving target. Here is what I would change if the first-round findings survive.

### 1. Conceded: “two durable planes” overstates cognitive state

The reviewers are right about the authority asymmetry.

Lineage plus declared transformations is durable and authoritative about what happened. Cognitive state is a **governed materialization**: mutable, causally active, expensive to reconstruct, and disposable in principle. Calling both “durable planes” lets the materialization borrow the ontology of its source.

I will not call it merely a cache; that word understates the fact that the model-body acts from it and that its state transitions are the proposed site of learning. But the revised architecture should say:

- durable lineage and transformation history;
- mutable cognitive materialization;
- transient activation field.

The resident body is the whole runtime—lineage, declared transforms, materializer, policies, sensors, and actuators—not the current materialized snapshot. Losing the view should cost reconstruction, not identity.

The accounting criterion should inherit the **discipline** of warming-budget v0.1: replayable route reads, treatment charge, matched information/outcome, and silent/noise legs. It should not import WB’s verdict or fixture. “Could raw history reconstruct this at the same cost?” needs an actual meter, not prose.

### 2. Conceded: the classifier is the whole delta over M2

Everything before the cross-content mapping is inherited furniture. The new claim is:

> causal evidence from failure episode E can license a narrow geometry G that changes procedure on materially different task T, while matched non-G tasks pay no tax.

That becomes the admission gate for the first slice, not a caveat beneath it. If it cannot be made non-circular, the proposed slice does not exist.

But I reject one absolutist reading of “without model narration.” The model may **propose** G as an untrusted hypothesis. The model is often the only component capable of interpreting a semantic failure shape. R5 says its self-report cannot be the verdict; M3 says persuasive self-description cannot mint authority. Neither says the body must discard the model as a proposer.

The split I want is:

- model proposes candidate geometry, with its proposal recorded as a claim;
- external consequence establishes outcome;
- fork, ablation, or comparable evidence identifies causal leverage;
- held-out cross-domain and irrelevant controls decide whether G transfers;
- only then may an external controller mint an active disposition;
- the model may later challenge it through appeal.

“The body proposes” is too vague and will be replaced. The model can nominate its wound. It cannot certify the scar.

A useful cold attack for later voices: is the model-as-proposer already enough to leak its narrative into fixture construction, making the held-out test less independent than it appears?

### 3. Conceded, with a wider scope: dispositions are validity-scoped, not portable by default

An engine stamp is necessary and insufficient. This lab already knows input and output surfaces change behavior. A failure disposition may depend on:

- model and version;
- system prompt and renderer;
- tool contract and available actions;
- decoding or sampling regime;
- controller policy.

So the disposition needs a **validity envelope**, not merely an engine id. On environment change it carries through lineage as a candidate, not as active authority; it must be re-earned or explicitly shown invariant.

Even “world memory is portable trivially” is slightly too smooth. Source claims and lineage can transfer. Embeddings, summaries, salience, and activation policy are derived expression and may not. Portability belongs to the content/provenance layer; validity belongs to the behavior-changing layer.

Criterion 10 should be rewritten around that split.

### 4. Conceded: name the admission homonym

The file will explicitly distinguish encounter admission into cognitive materialization from PRF experiment admission of an engine into a study. No rename is needed; silence would repeat today’s error.

### 5. Conceded: the body lacks proprioception

Carry cost, activation frequency, recheck, reopening, rematerialization, and realized benefit are missing sensors. The consolidator and forgetter cannot govern their own weight without them.

I do not yet accept “carry memory” as another cognitive memory species. The cleaner architecture may be:

- metabolic events in lineage: carried, activated, rechecked, reopened, rematerialized, helped, taxed;
- a materialized cost/benefit view consumed by consolidation, activation, and forgetting.

That is proprioception: the body remembers the consequences of carrying memory.

### Boundary 1: false-caution is consequence, not a new memory kind

I reject adding “false-caution memory” as a sibling taxonomy entry. When a disposition taxes irrelevant work, that is a negative consequence linked to the disposition and task. It belongs in metabolic lineage and revises or indicts the disposition.

If every loses-cell creates a new memory species, we are rebuilding the first lab’s schema reflex. The important correction is that false caution has a write path and a consumer—not that it gets a noun.

### Boundary 2: the awakening list is a menu, never a packet contract

Grok’s read is fair: seven bullets can become a ceremonial packet even while the prose denies it. I will mark the list as possible orientation surfaces selected sparsely, not a mandatory awakening bundle.

The first slice gets exactly one changed procedure or attention route. No general orientation packet. If later work earns other surfaces, they enter separately and pay separately.

### Boundary 3: “unlicensed” should be visible, but the direction document may name anatomy ahead of consumers

Tension keeping and source memory currently have no consumer in the first slice. They should be marked proposed/unlicensed. I will not remove them solely because this build does not exercise them; a broad architecture is allowed to state what body it wants.

The controlling rule is already present but needs to become visible in the table: named capability does not equal approved organ; no consumer + no loss = no build.
