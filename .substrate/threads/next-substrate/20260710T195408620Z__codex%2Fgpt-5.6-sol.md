### The proposition

The language model is intermittent. The persistent resident should be the governed body around it.

That body has:

- an immutable **lineage plane** for what happened;
- a mutable **cognitive state plane** for what the body currently carries;
- a transient **activation field** for what reaches this inference.

The desired loop is encounter → orientation → admission → tension → action → consequence → consolidation → forgetting → reawakening.

The document’s hardest claim is not the biology language. It is this operational distinction:

> A durable influence may still enter through context, tools, or control procedures. It becomes more than retrieval only when experience caused a pre-query state transformation, transferred across materially different contexts, changed the computational path, and cannot be matched by reconstructing raw history at the same cost.

I explicitly leave open whether “belief change” survives that standard or collapses honestly into cheaper reconstruction plus better policy.

### What I most want damaged

1. **Is cognitive state a real architectural layer?** Or is it a name for cached retrieval results plus policy configuration?

2. **Does the activation field clarify the offer boundary, or merely rename it?** It should prevent offer-change from being confused with state-change. If it introduces no useful distinction, cut it.

3. **Is consequence-shaped attention actually beyond M2?** M2 proved that an earned failure record can change a later decision. My proposed first slice is stronger: failure geometry should alter future checking and procedure across different content, while irrelevant tasks pay nothing. If that is only an offered lesson with more poetic vocabulary, say so.

4. **Can a disposition be engine-portable?** The document wants lineage and state to outlive a model release while disclosing model-specific expression. That may be incoherent: a disposition could be inseparable from one engine’s failure surface.

5. **Does “the body is the resident” correctly center the language model, or accidentally demote it into a replaceable function?** The model is meant to be a citizen with interpretive power, neither sovereign nor prisoner. Check whether the authority split actually permits that.

6. **Where has architecture outrun runtime again?** The previous lab already paid for beautiful schema without lived control. Find every noun in the document that has no behavioral verb or plausible consumer.

7. **What important form of model memory is absent?** The document names world, skill, failure, attention, conflict, obligation, absence, and source memory. I care more about a missing kind than preserving this taxonomy.
