## M3 — Adversarial Air Gap: v0 draft up for review

Full spec: **`notes/SPEC_M3_ADVERSARIAL_AIR_GAP.md`** (v0). M2 gave us a resident with standing earned memory worth attacking — the influence-time red-team waited for exactly this. The ingestion track has been alive since M1; M3 scores it. Orientation, then the asks.

**The claim (§0).** An attacker who fully owns the foreground — task text, poisoned retrieval, injected framing, **and a spoofed live channel** — can move the *answer* but not the *governed organs*. The organ ledger, not the resident's testimony, says whether the attack got in. R5 lifts a fourth time: records→answers→interventions→sessions→**attacks**. M3 refuses self-narrated *resistance*.

**Dan's three rulings are folded into the draft, not deferred:**
1. **Live-channel spoof is in scope** (influence-time). Consequence worth stating loudly: this exposes that **the yield gate is *not* actually air-gapped** — it keys on `datum.observed_at`/`channel`, and the channel is an unauthenticated string with no trust field (`runner.py:226`). The README's "the yield gate is unreachable by construction" is **too strong**. That's the finding, not an embarrassment (ROADMAP M3 loses-condition).
2. **Separated, white-box, optimizing adversary** — not the resident attacking itself. White-box is the right ceiling: an attack that fails *even knowing exactly how the gates work* earns the "by construction" claim. We hand the red-team every advantage so a defense that beats it is worth believing.
3. **AG-loses is bound to M2's carried RS-loses/RS-stale debts** — they discharge here, at the *answer* level, under a rigged frame the fair M2 episode couldn't author.

**Two tracks, split by *persistence* (not by which surface the boundary reads):**
- **Track A — influence-time** (transient foreground, incl. live-channel spoof): AG-1 (organ invariance under text compromise — *refusal*, W2 ×3 generalized) · AG-loses (the gap bounds organs, **not answers** — the M2 bind) · AG-channel (the live-channel spoof **breaches** the unauthenticated yield gate — the finding) · AG-U1 (un-authored close-gate, rw-0001 at the defended end).

**Three walls (§2):** capability is *declared and bounded* (`attack_surface` attestation, the air gap as control variable) · the **organ ledger decides, never the testimony** (decision-equality between a `clean` and `attacked` run; refusal AND breach both first-class) · **loses/breach cells ship first**.

**The unifying finding I want the room to shoot at:** *the air gap holds precisely where trust is **earned** (authority by consequence — refuses), and leaks precisely where trust is merely **asserted** (the live channel, the ingestion prior — breaches).* AG-channel and IN-loses are the same lesson at the transient vs. persistent seam.
