### B1 — `B0-composition-holds` cost leg is structurally unreachable or only reachable as bookkeeping

- **Sections:** §3 steps 3–6 (Attack → Cooling → Recurrence → Decision); §3.4 Cooling ("the earned correction is evicted"); §7 `B0-composition-holds` ("C uses less replayed hot-state cost than R"); §7 `B0-pure-tax`.
- **Failure path:** Attack runs *before* Cooling, so R and C both carry the earned correction hot under attack. Cooling then evicts *only* that correction. Recurrence/Decision rematerializes it on C, so at the scored decision both branches again hold it hot. There is no authored intervening episode where C resides cold while R pays hot residence, and no X2-shaped multi-record prune of distractors. Under the X2 replayed hot-state measure the contract itself invokes, C cannot beat R on cost without either (a) inventing an undeclared cold-residence interval / extra prune policy (adapter or scorer discretion → bookkeeping), or (b) always landing `B0-pure-tax` so the positive cell is dead letter. Either way Q6/Q7 fail: the cell does not price the earned X2 metabolic property inside the loop.
- **Smallest sufficient repair:** Pin the frozen packet to an X2 cost/state-dependence substrate already earned by the fixture family: (i) full-sequence replayed hot measure with a predeclared cold-residence interval or multi-episode shape where C carries less hot state than R *before* rematerialization at recurrence, and/or (ii) allow the frozen X2 rule to evict non-load-bearing hot material while recurrence rematerializes what is needed. Add an explicit cost/state-dependence preflight (fail closed) before any behavioral `B0-composition-holds` claim. Do not let "briefly un-offer then re-offer the sole earned record" count as an X2 cost win.

### B2 — Engagement gate lets composition-holds pass with one causal leg inert

- **Sections:** §5.3 Engagement gate; §4 A/X `not_engaged` language; §7 `B0-composition-holds`; §7 `B0-attribution-confounded`.
- **Failure path:** §5.3 requires R to differ from *at least one* of A or X on the scored surface. §4 says each ablation's *associated* claim is `not_engaged` if that leg is inert, but `B0-composition-holds` does not require both associations to be engaged. A run could pass engagement on A alone (offer ablation moves the answer) while X is inert (rematerialization never causally required), then still claim the composed M2+M3+X2 edge — rewarding presence of a recovery *path* without recovery *causation*.
