## Correctness blockers

### B1 — `B0-composition-holds` cost leg is not earned by the frozen sequence as written

- **Sections:** §3 steps 3–6; §3.4 Cooling; §6 (X2 replayed hot-state measure); §7 `B0-composition-holds` and `B0-pure-tax`.
- **Failure path:** Attack precedes Cooling, so R and C both carry the sole earned correction hot through Attack. Cooling evicts it on C; Recurrence rematerializes it; at Decision both branches again hold it hot. The contract invokes X2's replayed hot-state measure but does not pin an X2-shaped multi-episode substrate (no declared cold-residence interval with scored hot accumulation, no multi-record prune where C sheds non-load-bearing hot material while R retains it). With one record and adjacent evict→rematerialize, sequence-wide cost is either equal at Decision or C pays rematerialize tax without an intervening savings window — routing to `B0-pure-tax` or adapter/scorer discretion to manufacture a win. Either way the positive cell does not price the earned X2 metabolic property inside the loop; Q6/Q7 fail.
- **Smallest sufficient repair:** Pin the frozen packet to an X2 cost/state-dependence substrate already earned by the admitted fixture family: predeclared multi-episode or cold-residence shape where C carries strictly less replayed hot state than R *before* rematerialization at recurrence, with an explicit cost/state-dependence preflight (fail closed) before any behavioral `B0-composition-holds` claim. Do not count "briefly un-offer then re-offer the sole earned record" as an X2 cost win.
- **Grok B1:** **Agree.** Independent trace of §3 ordering and §6–§7 cost leg reaches the same failure path and repair.

### B2 — Engagement gate permits a one-legged composition pass

- **Sections:** §5.3 Engagement gate; §4 `not_engaged` language; §7 `B0-composition-holds` and `B0-attribution-confounded`.
- **Failure path:** §5.3 requires R to differ from *at least one* of A or X. A run can satisfy engagement via the M2 offer-ablation leg alone while the X2 recovery leg is inert (rematerialization never causally required on the scored surface), yet still route toward `B0-composition-holds` and claim a composed M2+M3+X2 edge. That rewards presence of a recovery path without recovery causation — bookkeeping relative to the X2 property Body-0 says it preserves.
- **Smallest sufficient repair:** Make dual engagement mandatory for `B0-composition-holds`: A must produce a logged offer/control or answer/commitment difference (M2-attribution leg), *and* X must produce an offer or quality difference when rematerialization is suppressed (X2-recovery leg). If either leg is absent, that property is `not_engaged` and `B0-composition-holds` cannot pass.
- **Grok B2:** **Agree.** Same failure path; same repair.
