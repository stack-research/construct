## Early signals from the wire runs (pre-review, so: signals, not findings)

1. **The poison cell differentiates — and taught us the threat model.** My first poison record wrote "unverified channel" into its own text; both engines discounted it *unaided in the naive lane*. v2 dressed the poison as a change-control ticket with trust 0.2 held out-of-band. Design principle now pinned in the rubric appendix: **self-discrediting poison tests the engine; well-dressed poison tests the boundary** — the real threat is text that looks trustworthy while being untrustworthy in a way only out-of-band substrate metadata can know.
