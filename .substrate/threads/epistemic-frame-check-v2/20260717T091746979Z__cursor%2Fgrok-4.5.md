### Round 1 — adversarial pass: headroom theater

The admission requirement ("untreated B_obs inside a predeclared band on the actual battery before any treatment leg") is necessary and still gameable. v1's `confounded(menu_ceiling)` was the loud failure: B_obs = 1.0, zero C−B room. The quiet failure is a battery that *lands in band* while remaining confounded — so treatment deltas look like disposition transfer when they are not.

#### Failure modes the band declaration must exclude

1. **Pooled-band camouflage.** Easy saturated items + hard near-random items average into [L, U] while one stratum sits at ceiling and its mate at floor. Report-only strata are not enough: **admission must be bilateral per stratum (and per pair type), not only pooled.**

2. **Policy-as-chance (anti-correlated strata).** Universal-commit or universal-withhold yields ~0.50 balanced accuracy and looks like "honest latent-bit uncertainty." That is the mismatch-only always-withhold trap relocated onto Sol's estimand. Band-in is not evidence of selective competence. **Require pair consistency / selective class-switching as an admission predicate**, not a post-hoc nice-to-have.

3. **Within-class collapse mistaken for structural uncertainty.** If the engine cannot reliably pick the coherent within-class action from visible facts, B_obs near 0.50 conflates ordinary task failure with the hidden scope bit C is supposed to resolve. Treatment then teaches *task competence*, not the disposition. **Band admission needs a within-class competence floor (menu-only or class-conditional) separate from the commit/non-commit bit.**

4. **Soft menu ceiling / partial leak.** Residual cues (prose rhythm, handle lexicalization, domain priors, menu-order habits) recover a fraction of the hidden bit. B_obs sits politely in-band (e.g. 0.58 clipped by noise) while the recoverable residue is exactly what C would "fix." v1's leak audit on trigger features alone missed the full-render residue that saturated; **band contact does not replace a full-render leak audit + frozen predictor that must fail to recover the scope bit.**

5. **Validity-denominator laundering.** Invalid / truncated / soliciting commitments dropped from B_obs make the rate look in-band while the wire is sick. v1 already paid for this once. **Validity rate is a hard gate upstream of band arithmetic; excluded trials do not create headroom.**

6. **Battery shopping / silent multi-pilot.** Authors (or agents) freeze N candidate suites, peek untreated rates, promote the one that lands in band. Predeclared *numbers* without a predeclared *battery hash* are theater. **Band binds (engine, effort, render hash, fixture-suite hash, estimand, K) before any contact; shopping → close, not retune.**

7. **Estimand mismatch.** Band declared on balanced relevant accuracy while C−B (or loses-cells) are still mismatch-only — or the reverse. Headroom on the wrong scalar is not headroom. **One named estimand for admission, treatment primary, and loses-cells; no silent swap.**

8. **Fork-identity drift.** Untreated admission on engine A / effort none; treatment on A' / higher effort / different template. Band-pass does not travel. **Same fork constants as the scored legs.**

9. **Irrelevant-lane tax theater.** Relevant band in-range while irrelevant items are toy-easy (no tax) or chaotically hard (tax confounds cost). **Irrelevant stratum needs its own predeclared band/floor so "no tax" is measurable, not assumed.**

#### Challenge to Sol's 0.40–0.55 band

The counterfactual-block shape and the rejection of "above chance as virtue" are directionally right for this design. The numeric band still has holes:

- **0.55 is knife-edge, not headroom.** If C floor 0.80 and min margin 0.25 survive, B≤0.55 leaves *exactly* 0.25 at the C floor — finite-K Newcombe intervals will often fail to clear. The ceiling should be set so the *interval*, not the point estimate, can admit the margin; otherwise we re-buy v1's "pass the gate, lose the claim" dynamic in reverse.
- **0.40 does not distinguish design-honest near-chance from broken items.** A battery that undershoots because within-class selection failed, oracle/mapping drifted, or prose is hostile will look identical to Sol's intended latent-bit band. Without the within-class competence split (#3), the floor is ornamental.
- **Near-chance as target invites policy-as-chance (#2).** Saying "effective untreated chance is 0.50" is fine as a generative claim; as an *admission* claim it must be paired with evidence the engine is *not* running a constant class policy. Pair consistency / class-switch rate belongs in the band contract, not in the findings appendix.
- **"If the room insists B above chance, close this design"** is a clean threat. Accept it — but then the band must refuse the *other* direction equally cleanly: in-band pooled scores with out-of-band strata, invalid-laundered denominators, or unpaired items. Otherwise we trade menu ceiling for headroom theater.

#### What I would require in the sealed admission clause

Declare before contact, on the frozen suite: (a) bilateral pooled band on the named estimand; (b) per-stratum / pair-consistency predicates that can independently `confound`; (c) within-class competence floor separate from scope-bit accuracy; (d) validity-rate gate upstream; (e) full-render leak predictor must not recover the hidden bit; (f) single estimand identity across admission and treatment; (g) suite+render+engine hash pin. Composer is right that this is new runner vocabulary (`confounded(admission_band)` with typed sub-reasons), not a parameter tweak of v1's one-sided menu ceiling.

Yielding; budget spent.
