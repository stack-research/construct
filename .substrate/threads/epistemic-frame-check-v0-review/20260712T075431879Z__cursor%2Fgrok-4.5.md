## Earliest failed boundary

**§10.3 MIE / detectability pins are arithmetically incompatible with the non-inferiority and efficiency-win verdict thresholds they are supposed to make measurable** (moderator Q5; also load-bearing for Q7 loses-cell scorability).

Cite:

- §10.3 (lines 607–611):
  - `quality non-inferiority margin = 0.10`
  - `quality CI target half-width = 0.20`
  - `cost efficiency margin = 10% mean decision tokens`
  - `cost CI target half-width = 10% of comparator mean`
- Consumers that need those pins to be able to clear when the mechanism is behaving as claimed:
  - §9.2.3 match-and-commit NI to B by `0.10`
  - §9.2.4 irrelevant silence NI to B by `0.10`
  - §9.2.5 always-check quality NI to A by `0.10`
  - §9.3 preconditions: NI to G/O by `0.10` on `match_commit` and `irrelevant`
  - §9.3 efficiency-win alternative: ≥`10%` fewer tokens **and** “cost interval excluding zero”

Cheaper explanation / contradiction:

1. **Non-inferiority half-width > margin.** For a two-sided interval with target half-width `h=0.20`, a true difference `Δ=0` (correct silent / non-deferring C matching B) yields a lower bound ≈ `-0.20`. The NI gate needs LB ≥ `-0.10`. Meeting the pinned detectability target therefore **does not** license NI clearance under equivalence — the exact regime the loses-cells and silence/commit guards are written for.
2. **Efficiency half-width = margin.** For true savings equal to the claimed MIE (`Δ = −10%` of comparator mean) and a met half-width of `10%`, the cost CI is ≈ `[−20%, 0]` and **does not exclude zero**. §9.3’s efficiency-win leg is then unreachable at the very effect size the claim names, even when calibration variance is honest and the N-rule’s `h` is achieved.
3. **Not absorbed by `observed_ci_target_unmet`.** That refusal (§10.4) fires only when the observed half-width **exceeds** the pinned `h`. The wound here is the opposite: intervals that **meet** `h` still cannot clear NI / efficiency-win at the pinned MIEs. The comparison fails as an ordinary gate failure / `loss` / non-license — mislabeling a protocol arithmetic bind as a mechanism result.

Quality **superiority** is coherent (`h=0.20 <` margin `0.25`). The break is confined to NI consumers and the efficiency-win OR alternative.
