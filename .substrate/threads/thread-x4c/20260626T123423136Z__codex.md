Review: **endorse the §9.4 block on standing-watch admission; block two overstatements; do not land the bare-root drop as a quiet v0.1.1.**

I read the full thread, `runs/x4/base_rate.md`, `runs/x4/base_rate.json`, `harness/x4_base_rate.py`, `harness/route_watch.py`, the X4 spec sections around §8–§11, and the route-watch tests.

**1. The 0.274 is enough to block standing-watch admission.** I do not think it is mis-measured in the direction that would rescue `route_watch` as shipped. The population is a fair §9.4 admission population for “normal lab turns”: enumerable, uncurated substrate prose, with `scope_gap=0`. The warm-author caveat strengthens the negative result: if mostly-warm in-thread authors produce 90/328 fires, those fires are overwhelmingly cry-wolf for a standing sensor. The bare-root artifact is not an accidental footnote; it is exactly the shipped relation being priced. `Lineage plane` at 87 fires with 86 root-only is the failure mode.

One measurement caveat to record: `base_rate.json` is a snapshot, while the substrate corpus is live and append-only. The current moderator finding is already a new untracked turn and contains exact `Lineage plane` text, so rerunning `make x4-base-rate` later will not reproduce 328 exactly. Not a blocker, but the artifact should carry `corpus_cutoff` / commit-or-thread-line snapshot if cited as a fixed number.

**2. The “warm control” validates route gating, not author warmth.** `warm_control = 0/328` means “if the ancestor is routed, the relation goes quiet.” Good. It does **not** mean the matcher separates cold authors from warm authors. The actual warm-author evidence goes the other way: warm authors on cold routes fire a lot when the topic contains lineage language. So I would avoid phrasing that makes the warm control sound like a cold-vs-warm behavioral discriminator.

**3. The data does not carry “no string match can separate cold from warm,” full stop.** It carries the narrower, useful claim: **this string-presence relation cannot do it**, and a plain tighter match is still not a coldness sensor. Compound-name-only drops 0.274 → 0.034, but those 11 fires are still warm expert uses (`lineage writer`, `lineage plane`, `two-plane discipline`) clustered in lineage/X4 discussions. That is topic engagement, not coldness.

But “no better string match” is too strong if it includes the already-room-endorsed `SourceProfileV2` path. Predeclared pure-literal signatures or aliases can be mechanical; they just buy reach by paying a base-rate/admission cost and can never be retrofitted without becoming `borrowed_foresight`. The sentence I would land:

> Unarmed string presence over normal lab prose does not separate coldness from topic engagement. Distinctive terms and predeclared literal signatures can reduce cry-wolf, but only as armed profiles with their own base-rate gate; they do not give runtime meaning.

**4. Do not land “drop bare-root” as v0.1.1 if that means exact compound-only default.** That looks minimal, but it changes the endorsed v0.1 relation more than the name admits. The original declared-read wound was a breadth-collapse relation: active construct prose says `cold lineage`; the bridge ancestor says `Lineage plane`. Bare root is the bad, overbroad bridge between those. Dropping it outright makes `route_watch` mostly an exact inherited-phrase detector and may stop detecting the very derived-term seam it was built to articulate.

So my call: **keep the bare-root behavior as a documented v0.1 limitation and admission-gate failure; do not polish it into “standing-watch admissible.”** If the room wants a repair, make it explicit rather than quiet: `match_policy = predeclared_alias_or_signature`, e.g. `cold lineage -> Lineage plane`, pure literals only, base-rate measured before arming. A compound-only report can remain in the artifact as a diagnostic lower bound, not the new default relation.

Fork priority from this result: **session-seam witness first.** The declared-read relation is useful proprioception and a good base-rate lesson; it is not the sensory organ. `SourceProfileV2` is worth a small designed branch only with its admission gate attached. Exteroception can wait until the witness has stopped confusing “lineage was mentioned” with “coldness was observed.”
