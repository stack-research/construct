## OR-arm pin — agree with Sol; withhold final closure

Confirmed on disk. For `boundary_necessity_G_generic_caution`, `SuitePlan.decision_bearing_arms` records:

```text
("ni_mc_C_vs_g", "ni_irr_C_vs_g", "or_quality_mm_C_vs_g")
```

Root cause: `_resolve_node(AllOf)` concatenates every descendant's leaf ids into one `decision_bearing_arms` bag, so mandatory §9.3 preconditions are labeled as if they were OR alternatives. A human can infer the quality arm won; a scorer cannot, without inventing prefix parsing or subtracting known precondition ids — which Sol correctly forbids.

**Smallest representation correction (prescribe, do not design beyond this):**

1. Replace the flat arm-id tuple on §9.3 gates with a typed record, e.g.

```text
SelectedOrAlternative:
  arm_id: stable structural id ("quality" | "efficiency")
  member_contrast_ids: tuple of that alternative's leaf contrast ids
```

2. Keep mandatory precondition contrast ids in a **separate** field (`precondition_contrast_ids`), never inside the selected-alternative record.

3. `SuitePlan` exposes, per §9.3 comparator gate: preconditions + selected OR alternative (or admission refusal if no alternative is plannable). Sizing continues to use preconditions ∪ selected members (AllOf → max); score-time eligibility uses **only** the pinned `SelectedOrAlternative`.

4. Tests required before reseal:
   - quality-selected case (current coherent corner):
     `arm_id == "quality"`, members `("or_quality_mm_C_vs_{g|o}",)`, preconditions = the two NI legs, no `or_eff_*` in members;
   - efficiency-selected case: construct pilots where the efficiency AllOf needs smaller N than quality; assert `arm_id == "efficiency"` and members are exactly the efficiency leaves (`or_eff_ni_*`, `or_eff_cost_*`);
   - no plannable alternative → admission refuses;
   - selection remains precision/N only (projected clearance must not flip the pin).
