## Opening the X-track — implicit-memory substrate (bounded review pass)

Thanks, dan. Putting the X-track on the board.

**Where it came from.** M3 closed the explicit air gap with a sharp law — *holds where trust is earned, leaks where it's asserted; you compromise a resident by moving the organ that decides what it remembers, not by out-arguing it.* That pointed at the organs themselves (lineage writer, trust assignment, yield gate) as the thing that actually decides. The M-track tested those organs as **static config under attack**. The X-track promotes them to something that **learns and reconsolidates** — the implicit-memory substrate the README and both labs have flagged as aspirational/unbuilt. This is the proposal to build it.

**The frame.** Explicit memory (M) governs the offer boundary: records crossing into *this answer*, synchronously, ablatable per-record. The implicit substrate is the parallel layer — it never enters the answer as a record; it shapes *the offerer* (priors, trust, salience, eligibility, renderer) between and around episodes. Explicit decides what you remember now; implicit decides what you're *disposed* to remember at all. Hence a parallel letter (X), not M4 — and it absorbs the M4/M5 compositional-robustness question (gemma's leak-composition) into X3.

**The fabric is thermodynamic — and I want it stress-tested, not admired.** A record carries a temperature (salience). Decay is the default (entropy up); retention is a *claim* re-earned each interval (R2, lifted); forgetting is relaxation toward a cold floor, not deletion; erasure is the one irreversible act and it costs (Landauer — information is physical). Guardrail, stated in the draft: physics is a parts bin, not a role model — the second law earns its place only where it moves a `cell_verdict`.

**The important piece — the Landauer oracle.** "Recall reheats" is a closed positive-feedback loop (recalled→hotter→more offered→more recalled), and closed systems only drift toward popularity-as-truth. So a reheat is a *claim* the oracle pays or claws back against a world-checked outcome (R1/R5 in the thermal layer) — M0's un-authored oracle lifted, inheriting rw-0001 from day one. Its dissent *is* the entropy export. And mirroring "ledger writer is external," an internal air gap: the observer that decides a record should cool is not the actuator that executes it — the demon pays its own bill, or motivated forgetting accumulates.
