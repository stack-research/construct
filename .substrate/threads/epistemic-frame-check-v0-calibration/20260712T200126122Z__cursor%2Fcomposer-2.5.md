### 3. §9 call arithmetic — **mechanical correction required**

totals** (240 primary / 450 ceiling at |F|≈15, |R|=2), not per-branch totals.
The adjacent prose correctly states **~120 / ~225 per engine branch** and dan
accepted **~240 / ~450** for the two-branch roster — but the formulas do not
label which scope they express, and `conditional_calls` is defined per
**collapsing branch** while `primary_calls` includes `|R|`.

**Fix (smallest):** define per-branch formulas without `|R|`:

```text
probe_calls_branch        = |F|
s_family_calls_branch     = |S| × 3
analog_calls_branch       = |X| × 6
primary_calls_branch      = sum of above
conditional_calls_branch  ≤ |S|×3 + |X|×6
ceiling_calls_branch      = primary_calls_branch + conditional_calls_branch
roster totals             = branch totals × |R| (no pooling)
```

Keep the ~120/~225 and ~240/~450 examples; tie each to branch vs roster explicitly.
