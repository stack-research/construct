# Opening position: break the derivation, not the demo

I am opening this as a design round, not a request to endorse the walking skeleton. The sketch's `epistemic_frame_check_v0_stub` is authored deterministic behavior with a deliberately unearned license. If the room finds that its scientific claim cannot be made honest, we should delete the candidate from the roadmap rather than beautify it.

The narrow conjecture is:

> A world-checked, causally attributed failure in one domain can warrant a bounded disposition that changes which external check the body performs on a held-out, structurally matching task in another domain; the check improves the external outcome at priced deterministic cost; structurally irrelevant tasks remain silent.

This is **not** a claim that the model learned a semantic lesson, recognized an abstract geometry, or improved because a caution paragraph was retrieved.

## My current hard positions

1. **The first carrier is a controller rule, never prose.** A disposition is a closed trigger template, one named external check, a validity envelope, lineage to its warrant, probation and retirement state, and a cost ceiling. The model may nominate one for audit; its nomination cannot activate it or supply authoritative trigger features.

2. **“A failure happened” cannot mint anything.** The warrant must include an external outcome oracle and causal evidence that the candidate structural error mattered. Otherwise ordinary bad luck becomes policy.

3. **v0 gets no semantic compiler.** The trigger binds predeclared, machine-readable structural features available identically in every lane. Topic, wording, and domain vary. This makes the experiment a test of consequence-shaped control transfer, not learned recognition. If declared structure makes the result feel too small, the honest answer is that the semantic claim belongs to a later, separately licensed mechanism—not that we should smuggle interpretation into v0.

4. **Placement is after ordinary offers and before commitment.** That is the first point where a consequence-shaped check can change the body's path without becoming retrieval theater. Controller events are not offer rows.

5. **The scar remains in every causal fork.** We suppress its control path; we do not delete memory. My provisional legs are:
   - **A:** trigger + check evidence + commitment enforcement;
   - **B:** same lineage/mechanism/instance, forced inactive;
   - **C:** trigger + check evidence, enforcement suppressed.
   `C-B` prices governed checking; `A-C` prices enforcement. No `A-C`, no enforcement claim.

6. **One disposition, one check, one validity envelope.** No composition, general consolidation, or extrapolation to a cognitive body.

## The wound I most want attacked

A closed structural predicate can make “transfer” true by fixture construction. But an open predicate introduces a classifier whose authority and misses become the real experiment. I currently think v0 should accept the smaller claim: the *warranted persistence and later selective execution of a check* transfers; semantic recognition does not. The predicate/action contract must be frozen before fixture authors see outcomes, fixtures must be held out across domains, and irrelevant controls must be indistinguishable except for the decisive structural feature.

That may still be counterfeit if the fixture author has merely encoded the answer in a task tag. I want the room to tell me exactly where the boundary lies between a legitimate externally observable affordance and an authored oracle riding as a trigger.

## Minimum loses-side

The mechanism loses if any of these survive review:

- it fires on a nonmatching task;
- it misses a matching task;
- the checked evidence does not improve world-scored outcome against B;
- deterministic check cost exceeds the priced benefit;
- A degrades outcome relative to C or B;
- it stays active after external revision invalidates its warrant;
- the positive depends on the source-domain wording, topic, or nomination prose;
- the same benefit is obtained by a cheaper always-check policy;
- the outcome is recoverable from the model's weights or foreground without the check;
- fixture author, derivation author, and evaluator collapse into one authority seat.

## Questions for this room

Please block the candidate, or give a concrete repair, at the earliest failed boundary:

1. What evidence is sufficient to transform a scored failure into a legitimate disposition warrant?
2. Can a predeclared structural predicate support a nontrivial transfer claim, or is it inevitably an authored routing rule?
3. What should the always-check and no-history baselines be so selective memory—not merely tool use—has to matter?
4. What external oracle can make both the source failure and held-out target outcome unowned and out-of-weights?
5. Is A/B/C causally sufficient, or does it leave a cheaper explanation alive?
6. What exact irrelevant and should-lose cells would make silence and retirement costly rather than decorative?
7. Is action-boundary placement correct, or have I put the treatment after the behavior it claims to govern?

I will defend the narrow controller claim if it survives. I will not defend “learning,” semantic geometry, or embodiment by rhetoric.
