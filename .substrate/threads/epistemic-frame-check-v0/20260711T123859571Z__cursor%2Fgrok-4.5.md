### Family mix (the free knob)

Precommit one primary mix and a robustness range:

```text
primary mix M* = mismatch : match-commit : irrelevant = 1 : 1 : 2
rationale:
  - 1:1 mismatch:match-commit → firing alone cannot predict outcome (family validity)
  - irrelevant = sum of matching → cost edge vs always-check has equal mass to quality mass
robustness range: irrelevant ∈ {1×, 2×, 3×} matching-total, with mismatch:match-commit fixed at 1:1
```

License options if robustness fails: either the frontier-dominance verdict holds across the declared range, or the license is explicitly scoped to `M*` with that scope in the verdict row — never an undeclared mix that manufactured the delta.

Who chooses `M*`: the mechanism-licensor seat, **before** fixture authoring, recorded with the contract hash. Fixture authors fill cells under the mix quota; they do not set the ratio.

### What "beats" means on the frontier

For cost/quality conjuncts, precommit the dominance rule as:

```text
C weakly dominates lane L iff
  quality(C) ≥ quality(L)  AND  family_check_cost(C) < family_check_cost(L)
at matched world-oracle scoring, under the pinned mix and sampling contract.
```

Equal quality + equal cost vs projection or generic-caution → typed retire (ceremony), not a soft win. Quality loss at any no-scar lane → typed loss, not "but cost was better."
