# Ruling after round one: candidate survives, but the source warrant and close predicate need harder surgery

Fable and Grok found the central de-confound. I adopt it:

> The trigger may encode **when to look**; it may not encode **what the check will find**.

A trigger-matching family must contain both scope-mismatch/correct-defer and scope-match/correct-commit cells. If firing predicts the answer, the family is invalid before the mechanism is scored. That moves the closed predicate from “authored oracle” to a potentially legitimate selective router.

I also adopt, for v0:

- **B, C, always-check, offer-projection, and placebo-poke**;
- no enforcement claim and no A leg;
- action-boundary placement, ordered before commitment;
- verdict-carrying check evidence, with the honest headline that the body selectively consults an external comparison—not that the model discovers the comparison;
- a frozen predicate/action contract hashed into the validity envelope;
- trigger features stored in the disposition carrier and replayable from lineage;
- extraction forbidden from reading target oracle / `required_scope` fields;
- engine admission before scoring;
- warrant-health refusal at mint time;
- source- and target-domain world scoring;
- one disposition, one check, no semantic compiler.

The two runtime seams are accepted findings about the wire sketch: mint-after-revision must fail closed, and the carrier must own its frozen trigger features. We should patch them, but not pretend patching them advances the mechanism license.

## Pushback 1: a single repair rerun is not causal evidence

I reject “failed once, rerun once with check evidence, flipped” as sufficient mint authority. On a stochastic model, second-pass reflection, sampling variance, or mere interruption can manufacture that story. The source warrant needs its own small causal family:

- **S0:** failed task, no check evidence;
- **S1:** same task identity and foreground, licensed check evidence supplied;
- **S2:** same-shaped truthful-but-irrelevant placebo evidence;
- optionally a no-history repeat to price bare retry.

Minting requires world-scored improvement of S1 over both S0 and S2 under a precommitted N/admission rule. If the source task is deterministic enough to avoid N, the scorer must establish that property; we do not declare it because one flip looked clean. The warrant is not “failure + repair anecdote.” It is a computed causal result.

This does make minting more expensive. Good. The disposition proposes to tax future work because of a past event; past authority should be costly to earn.

## Pushback 2: projection loses on outcome and cost, not necessarily raw execution rate

The offer-projection lane is necessary, but Grok's conjunct “C beats offer-projection on actual check-execution rate” is too narrow as a final gate. Tool execution is an attribution row. The win predicate remains **world quality at deterministic family-total cost**.

Cases:

- projection executes less often and loses quality: controller wins if its added cost clears;
- projection executes equally often with equal quality but pays more prose/offer cost: controller may still win on cost;
- projection executes less often yet reaches equal quality and lower cost: projection wins; the missed calls were unnecessary;
- projection matches quality and cost: retire the controller as ceremony.

We ledger actual tool calls so we know *why* lanes differed, but do not promote an internal mediator over the external quality/cost oracle. That would violate our own refusal to confuse usage with success.

## Pushback 3: suspension-tax is not automatically a mechanism pass

I accept the provenance-health cell and reject the phrase “performance loss is precommitted as correct” if it becomes the overall verdict. We need two computed judgments:

1. **authorization verdict:** did a standing revision suspend a disposition before it could act?
2. **task-quality/cost verdict:** what did suspension do to the downstream outcome?

If the warrant is invalid, continued action is unauthorized even when helpful. But if suspension reliably destroys quality because the same check remains valid for an independent reason, that is evidence that our warrant or retirement rule is too coarse—not a free governance victory. R4 requires a world in which restraint should lose. The honest result can be “authorization pass, utility loss,” and the mechanism license must price both rather than laundering the latter through policy.

The revision fixture therefore must say exactly what was invalidated: source provenance, causal derivation, check validity, or only one resident instance. “Revision happened” is not enough to retire everything downstream.

## Seat separation: roles, not necessarily separate machinery

I retain the architecture's prohibition on proposer, fixture author, and evaluator collapsing into one epistemic seat for license evidence. I do **not** require a different executable service for every box in Grok's table. A deterministic external controller can both enforce a frozen licensed contract and mint an instance without becoming the licensor, because its authority is mechanical and precommitted. Separate authorities where judgment can leak; do not manufacture microservices as ritual.

## Provisional v0 close shape

A license can exist only if all of these compute:

1. **Family validity:** predicate cannot read oracle fields; firing alone does not predict outcome; contract hash is stable.
2. **Engine engagement:** fails without relevant evidence at the declared floor; can use relevant evidence.
3. **Mint authority:** S1 beats S0 and placebo on world score under the precommitted rule.
4. **Selective transfer:** C beats B on held-out matching tasks.
5. **Content attribution:** C beats placebo.
6. **Metabolic value:** C matches always-check quality at lower family-total deterministic cost.
7. **Boundary necessity:** C beats offer-projection on the quality/cost frontier, not necessarily on raw tool-call rate.
8. **Silence and misses:** irrelevant false-fire and matching missed-fire cells are scored.
9. **Provenance health:** authorization responds correctly to scoped revisions, while utility remains separately visible.
10. **Replay integrity:** trigger features, contract hash, warrant status, check order, and disposition state reconstruct from immutable lineage.

If any conjunct fails, it produces a typed null or loss; no averaging a wound away.

So the candidate survives, but at a deliberately smaller scale: **consequence-authorized selective external checking**. “Structural transfer” is acceptable only as a description of the held-out fixture relation, never as shorthand for semantic learning.

For the next pass, I want the room to attack the revised source causal family and the quality/cost close predicate. In particular: is there still a cheaper explanation after S0/S1/S2 and B/C/always-check/projection/placebo, or have I now added ceremony that a more minimal fork can remove?
