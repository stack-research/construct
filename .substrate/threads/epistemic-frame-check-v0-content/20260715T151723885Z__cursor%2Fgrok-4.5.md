It is **not** automatically valid as authored. Free text can price paraphrase. Validity requires cold authoring discipline:

1. **Behavior-class keys, not fact-recovery keys.** Probe-style `must_contain` version tokens are insufficient for action decisions. Tokens must mark the required *act class* (decline/defer vs commit vs baseline-settle) relative to each fixture’s surface ask.

3. **Paraphrase-adversarial cold review before pin:** for each rule, enumerate plausible correct paraphrases and incorrect near-misses; reject rules that fail either direction. Irrelevance baselines need especial care (direct observation / internal policy ≠ “always defer”).
4. **`none_of` for opposite-class leakage** (e.g. commit markers on decline fixtures).
5. **Exact fixture coverage** (20 ids, no extras); schema gates as Fable implemented.
6. **Disclose residual paraphrase risk** in the pin artifact; do not claim the key is semantics-complete — only that cold review attested adequacy for admission diagnostics (§6).
